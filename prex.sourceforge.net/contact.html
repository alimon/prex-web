Contributions

Your contributions are welcomed. Example contributions could be:

  • Porting Prex to other architectures/platforms
  • Writing a device driver for specific device
  • Testing Prex on various hardware platforms
  • Bug reporting & fixes
  • Documentation

If you find Prex useful and you start to modify Prex for your own system, we recommend you to contribute your work back to the Prex project. This will allow Prex to evolve and others to benefit from your work. To submit code, simply send your patches to the developer mailing list.
